A legendary serpentine lake monster reported to inhabit Lake Simcoe in Ontario, Canada, also known as 'Beaverton Bessie' or 'Kempenfelt Kelly.' The creature is described as measuring 12-70 feet in length with a charcoal-gray body and dog or horse-like head.
