A cryptozoological creature from the Canary Islands, specifically the Anaga massif region of Tenerife, described as an enormous hairy eel that allegedly inhabited a pool at Punta de Anaga. According to local legends, the creature had a head covered in hair with a threatening appearance and terrorized nearby villagers.
