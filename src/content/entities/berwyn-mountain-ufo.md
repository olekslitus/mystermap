On 23 January 1974, a massive explosion and brilliant lights in the sky over the Berwyn Mountains sent residents of Llandrillo rushing into the night convinced a plane had crashed. District nurse Pat Evans drove toward the summit expecting crash victims but encountered instead a large, pulsating sphere of orange and red light on the hillside. Official investigations attributed the event to a coincident magnitude-3.5 earthquake and a bright meteor, but UFO researchers have long alleged a military recovery operation took place on the mountain that night, earning it the nickname the Welsh Roswell.
