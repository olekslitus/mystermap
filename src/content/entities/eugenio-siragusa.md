Eugenio Siragusa (1919–2006) was a Sicilian customs official who became one of Italy's most prominent UFO contactees after claiming his first encounter in 1952. He described ongoing communications with extraterrestrial beings focused on spiritual development and nuclear disarmament, founding the 'Cosmic Brotherhood' movement. His encounters — especially a 1962 meeting with two silver-suited aliens on Mount Etna — attracted followers throughout Italy and abroad. He wrote multiple books and gave lectures until the end of his life.
