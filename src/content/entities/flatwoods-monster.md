On the night of September 12, 1952, three boys in Flatwoods, West Virginia
saw something fall from the sky onto a nearby hilltop. They climbed up
with two adults and a dog, and there — illuminated by their flashlight —
was a ten-foot-tall figure in what looked like a green metallic suit, its
spade-shaped head crowned by glowing orange eyes. The thing hissed, glided
toward them, and the group fled. Several were sick for hours afterward.

Skeptics blame an owl, a meteor, or both. The Flatwoods Monster appeared
once, was gone by morning, and has had a holiday in town ever since.
