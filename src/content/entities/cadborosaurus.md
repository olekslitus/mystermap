A legendary sea serpent, nicknamed 'Caddy,' reported to inhabit the waters off British Columbia, Canada. The creature has been described as a massive serpentine beast with a horse-like head, approximately 40 feet or longer in body length, featuring a columnar body, prominent eyes, and a finned, spiny tail.
