An Irish and Scottish cryptid from folklore, whose name means 'water hound' or 'hound of the deep.' The creature is traditionally described as resembling both a dog and an otter, standing 10-15 feet tall with a white pelt, black ear tips, and a distinctive black cross-shaped marking on its back.
