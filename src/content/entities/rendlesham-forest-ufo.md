In late December 1980, USAF personnel stationed at RAF Woodbridge reported a series of encounters with an unidentified craft in Rendlesham Forest, Suffolk. Sergeant Jim Penniston claimed to have touched a triangular craft covered in hieroglyphic symbols, while Deputy Base Commander Lt Col Charles Halt documented a second night's encounter on audio tape and in an official memo released under the US Freedom of Information Act in 1983. It remains the UK's most thoroughly documented and debated UFO case, with multiple military witnesses, a declassified government memo, and physical trace evidence on the forest floor.
