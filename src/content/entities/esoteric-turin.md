Turin occupies a singular position in European esoteric tradition: it is simultaneously a vertex of the White Magic Triangle (with Prague and Lyon) and the Black Magic Triangle (with London and San Francisco). The city sits at the confluence of the Po and Dora rivers, rivers traditionally identified with solar and lunar energies. Piazza Castello represents its 'white soul' to the east; Piazza Statuto — built over an ancient execution site and cemetery — represents its 'black heart' to the west. Occultists have been drawn to Turin for centuries.
