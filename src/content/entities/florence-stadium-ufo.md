On October 27, 1954, a football match in Florence's Stadio Artemio Franchi was interrupted when some 10,000 spectators simultaneously looked up to watch a formation of cigar-shaped objects cross the sky and hover above the stadium. Players and fans flooded onto the pitch. After the objects departed, a silvery fibrous material — described as 'angel hair' — drifted down from above. Samples were collected and analysed, revealing an unusual mineral composition. The event remains one of the best-documented mass UFO sightings in European history.
