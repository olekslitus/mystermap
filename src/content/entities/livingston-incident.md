On 9 November 1979, forestry worker Robert Taylor encountered a large dark sphere hovering in a forest clearing on Dechmont Law, near Livingston. Two smaller spiked spheres rolled toward him, seized his trouser legs, and dragged him toward the main object before he lost consciousness. He awoke with torn clothing, grazes, and a severe headache; police found ladder-shaped ground impressions at the scene. Lothian and Borders Police recorded the matter as a criminal assault — making it the only UFO encounter in British legal history to be the subject of a formal police criminal investigation.
