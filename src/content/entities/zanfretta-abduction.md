Pier Fortunato Zanfretta, a security guard from Genoa, reported the first of eleven alleged alien abductions on December 6, 1978, near the hamlet of Marzano in the Ligurian hills. His case is considered one of the most thoroughly investigated in European UFO research, involving hypnotic regression, polygraph testing, physical evidence at the encounter site, and detailed accounts of the entities he encountered — which he described as reptilian beings over three metres tall. The consistency of his testimony across years of questioning continues to fascinate researchers.
