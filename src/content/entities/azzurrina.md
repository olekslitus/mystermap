Azzurrina is the ghost of Guendalina, an albino child living in Montebello Castle in 14th-century Emilia-Romagna. Because albinism was considered a mark of the devil, her parents dyed her hair dark — a blue tinge gave rise to her nickname. On June 21, 1375, she rolled her ball into the cellar and was never seen again. Every five years on the summer solstice, paranormal investigators descend on the castle and record sounds the scientific community has not been able to fully explain.
