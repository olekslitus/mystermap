Since 1992, the small town of Bonnybridge in the Falkirk council area has accumulated more UFO sightings per capita than almost anywhere on Earth, earning the designation world number-one hotspot from ufologists. The Falkirk Triangle stretches from Stirling to the outskirts of Edinburgh and into Fife, with an estimated 300 sightings reported annually by the mid-1990s. Local councillor Billy Buchanan documented over 200 incidents in 1992 alone and wrote to multiple UK Prime Ministers demanding an official investigation, receiving no substantive response.
