Dulce is a quiet town in northern New Mexico, on the Jicarilla Apache
reservation. In the early 1980s, conspiracy theorist Paul Bennewitz
became convinced that beneath nearby Archuleta Mesa was a seven-level
joint human-alien underground laboratory operated, in part, by reptilian
beings. His sources were misinterpreted military signals; his beliefs
spread anyway.

Dulce Base has since become a fixture of American conspiracy lore: the
imagined infrastructure beneath the desert, the hybrid experiments, the
firefight on Level Six. Locals will tell you that the strangest thing
about Dulce is how many strangers come asking.
